Getting there & staying

Travel & accommodations

Flying in

Tucson International Airport (TUS) is an easy 20-minute drive to and from downtown Tucson.

If you fly into Phoenix Sky Harbor (PHX) you can either rent a car or book a seat on a shuttle to and from downtown Tucson; the drive takes 1.5–2 hours depending on traffic.

Getting to the farm

Agua Linda Farm is about a 45-minute drive south of downtown Tucson. We're providing optional free shuttles for guests both ways on Saturday, so you don't need to worry about driving or parking.

Agua Linda Farm on Google Maps  ·  farm website

Where to stay

For out-of-town guests, we'll be staying in the Barrio Viejo neighborhood of downtown Tucson all weekend and would love to have you nearby. It's walkable, near lots of good food, bars, and artsy stuff, and it's where the shuttles will pick up and drop off.
