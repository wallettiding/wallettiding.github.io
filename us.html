Us

Chad & Rebekah

"The right decision… at the wrong time…"

Yes, we're already married. And at the time, it was a difficult decision for us. As most of you know, we were forced to take a leap of faith together when we decided to move to Saudi Arabia. Marriage wasn't something we were planning on then, but we knew we had a connection worth holding onto, worth trying to build a life around. It was one of the hardest decisions we've made together and ended up being one of the best.

As tempting as it was to drop the whole family album for everyone to peruse, we kept it to photos mostly featuring the two of us; where we've lived, where we've traveled, and how we've grown together over these last seven years. There's our life in Tucson, a brief stint in Flagstaff and Kansas, our years in Saudi Arabia, and the many trips back home and around the world.

We both tend to point the camera outward; at the spaces, the things, and the people around us. But this day, and this celebration, are about us, so we're turning the lens around!

Photos only ever capture the highlights, leaving out the mundane and difficult parts of building a life far from home; but they still help us remember some of the best and most meaningful times of our lives. Looking back, we're reminded how fortunate we've been to be surrounded by friends, family, and a couple of dogs who tolerated all the moving. There's plenty worth sharing here.

Now it's time to celebrate the right decision with all of our people!
